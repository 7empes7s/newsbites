Spring 2026 anime is arriving as a cluster of recognizable titles rather than a
single dominant launch. If you want the short version of the season, start
here.

## Spring 2026 shortlist

These five titles are the clearest early watchlist for the season.

- **Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- Season 4**  
  April 8, 2026 at 23:00 JST. The biggest returning title in this first-wave
  group.
- **Classroom of the Elite 4th Season: Second Year, First Semester**  
  April 1, 2026. One of the strongest sequel draws of the season.
- **That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Season 4**  
  April 2026, with two consecutive cours. The widest runway in this shortlist.
- **Daemons of the Shadow Realm**  
  April 4, 2026 at 23:30 JST. A major new fantasy adaptation entering the same
  early window.
- **Agents of the Four Seasons: Dance of Spring**  
  March 28, 2026 at 24:00 JST, effectively March 29. The prestige opener that
  starts the season before April fully arrives.

## Why this list matters

This is not the entire spring calendar. It is the cleaner watchlist for the
first-wave titles most likely to shape the conversation quickly across global
anime audiences.

What makes the season interesting is not only the number of releases. It is the
mix: a large fantasy sequel, a major school-drama return, another heavyweight
franchise in *Slime*, one big new adaptation, and one prestige early starter.

## Why it matters

Spring 2026 looks strong because several recognizable titles are landing within
days of each other. The season feels active early because it is being driven by
several headline releases at once, not by one runaway launch.
