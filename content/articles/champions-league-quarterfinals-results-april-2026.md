The 2025/26 UEFA Champions League semi-finalists are confirmed after a dramatic night of quarter-final second legs.

## Tuesday, April 14 — First legs complete

### PSG vs Liverpool — PSG win 4-0 on aggregate

The reigning champions eased past Liverpool. Ousmane Dembélé scored twice at Anfield to seal a 4-0 aggregate victory. Liverpool, despite being the Premier League champions, could not muster a response.

**Score on the night:** Liverpool 0-2 PSG  
**Aggregate:** 0-4 to PSG

### Atletico Madrid vs Barcelona — Atletico win 3-2 on aggregate

Barcelona threw everything at Atletico at the Metropolitano but fell short. Barcelona scored twice in the first half through Lamine Yamal and Ferran Torres, but Ademola Lookman's goal seven minutes later proved decisive.

**Score on the night:** Atletico Madrid 1-2 Barcelona  
**Aggregate:** 3-2 to Atletico

## Wednesday, April 15 — Second legs complete

### Arsenal vs Sporting CP — Arsenal win 1-0 on aggregate

Arsenal progressed with the minimum of drama. A 1-0 lead from the first leg was enough as Mikel Arteta's side nullified Sporting at the Emirates. The game ended 0-0, sending Arsenal into their first Champions League semi-final since 2019.

**Score on the night:** Arsenal 0-0 Sporting CP  
**Aggregate:** 1-0 to Arsenal

### Bayern Munich vs Real Madrid — Bayern win 6-4 on aggregate (THRILLER)

The tie of the round delivered. Bayern won an incredible match 4-3 on the night, advancing 6-4 on aggregate after a 4-3 second-leg result. Real took an early lead through Arda Guler after Manuel Neuer's mistake. The game see-sawed with goals from Mbappé (2), Kane, and Guler again, but two late goals from Michael Olise and Luis Díaz sealed it for Bayern. Real finished with nine men — Eduardo Camavinga and Arda Guler were sent off.

**Score on the night:** Bayern Munich 4-3 Real Madrid  
**Aggregate:** 6-4 to Bayern

## What the semi-finals promise

**Arsenal vs Atletico Madrid** — This is the contrast tie: Arsenal's attacking flair against Atletico's defensive discipline. Arsenal reached the semis for the first time in seven years, while Atletico are back after their 2017 run to the final.

**PSG vs Bayern Munich** — The heavyweight matchup. Both teams hunt the trophy, both dropped significant first-leg results. Bayern's 4-3 win over Real was the statement result — they have not lost to Real in knockout rounds in two visits now.
