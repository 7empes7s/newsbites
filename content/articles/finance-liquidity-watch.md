## The shift underneath the noise

When rates stay meaningfully positive, the market stops rewarding vague future
stories as easily as it did during ultra-cheap money.

That changes what gets attention.

## What investors are looking for

- clearer balance sheets
- cleaner cash conversion
- fewer moving parts in the core business

## Why it matters

This does not mean growth is dead. It means growth now has to survive a more
skeptical environment.

For operators, that usually translates into a simpler rule: if the business
model needs a heroic macro backdrop to work, the market will eventually price
that fragility in.
