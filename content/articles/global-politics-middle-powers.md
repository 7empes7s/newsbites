## Why this matters now

Global politics is no longer explained cleanly by a single rivalry. Power is
still concentrated, but influence is spreading into more hands.

## What middle powers can do

- broker specific deals without carrying the full burden of superpower conflict
- shift supply chains through ports, finance, and energy access
- extract concessions by staying flexible rather than fully aligned

## What to watch

The key signal is not rhetoric. It is whether these states can repeatedly turn
their geographic or commercial position into durable negotiating power.
