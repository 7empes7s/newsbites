The 2025/26 UEFA Champions League semi-finals begin with a heavyweight clash as Real Madrid host Bayern Munich at the Bernabeu in what is arguably the most anticipated matchup of the tournament.

## Recent Form

Real Madrid come into this match on the back of a 3-1 victory over Athletic Bilbao in La Liga, with Jude Bellingham scoring twice. The defending champions have won their last three home games in all competitions.

Bayern Munich are also in excellent form, having won their last four matches including a 4-2 derby victory over Augsburg. Their away form has been particularly impressive, winning five of their last six away games.

## Key Matchups

### Bellingham vs Kane
England internationals go head-to-head as Bellingham looks to add to his 12 Champions League goals this season against his former teammate.

### Courtois vs Neuer
Two of the world's best goalkeepers will be crucial in determining the outcome. Both have been in inspired form this campaign.

## Head-to-Head

Real Madrid have historically had the upper hand against German opponents in knockout ties, winning 15 of their last 20 quarter-final and semi-final matches against Bundesliga clubs.

However, Bayern beat Real in the 2020 quarter-finals and will be confident of causing an upset.

## Prediction

This promises to be an open, attacking contest. Real Madrid's home advantage and experience in these situations just edges it, but expect goals from both sides.
